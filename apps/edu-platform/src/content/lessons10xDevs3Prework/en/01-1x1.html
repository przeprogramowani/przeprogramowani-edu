What can AI do in 2026?

AI is developing so fast that many of today's top experts in the field tend to underestimate the scope of what will be possible even in a matter of a few years. In 2026, my bet is that AI will be even more powerful than you imagine right now.

At the same time, AI’s impact will depend a lot on applications and infrastructure developed on top of the core AI “engines”—the machine learning models as we now call them. In big tech companies, the AI models play a similar role as your phone’s operating system, which on its own is not so impressive, but combined with apps and hardware turns into a very powerful tool.

Therefore, what you do next will be decisive. If you just experiment with ChatGPT-style bots as a consumer, you will be amazed for a while, but you might not get far. However, if you start building your own tools, workflows, and integrations, and at the same time understand how the core AI engines work, then you will be on the front edge of the coming transformation.

This lesson is to prepare you for that forward-looking perspective. The goal of this lesson is to:

  1. Give you a bird’s-eye view of what’s coming in AI and related fields in the next few years.
  2. Help you understand what AI can and cannot do.
  3. Show you a few examples of AI-powered workflows and integrations.
  4. Discuss the main technical and ethical challenges in building AI solutions.
