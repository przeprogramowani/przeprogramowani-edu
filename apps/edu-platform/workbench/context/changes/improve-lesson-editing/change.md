## Notes

We have this flow of: /lesson-draft, /lesson-editor-pl and /lesson-rc-review to work on lesson-drafts. Often when I use this flow I still have to do a lot of editorial work. Mostly to get rid, trim down or edit sentences that dont bring value, say the same thing, in other words (and sometimes using the exact same words), mostly as narrative figures. Additionaly much of this is trying to hard to keep the narration between lessons and between lesson sections, instead of optimizing for clarity. Also we often bring new concepts in the lessons, and they're not clearly explained - they're introduced in half-way manner, without: what is this and why its important. Each of the skills have some elements that try to prevent this but it doesnt work as a whole.
